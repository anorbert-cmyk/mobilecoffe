# Barista Hustle - Monetization Model Research

## Company Overview
- **Industry Leader**: Coffee industry's leading online barista training provider
- **Founder**: Matt Perger (two-time world champion barista)
- **Target Audience**: Professional baristas, coffee shop owners, home enthusiasts

## Pricing Structure (2026 Data)

### Individual Plans
1. **Monthly Subscription**: $18/month
   - Access to all online courses & certifications
   - Access to all premium content
   - Certifications that never expire
   - Global network of coaches
   - 30-day money-back guarantee
   
2. **Annual Subscription**: $15/month (billed annually = $180/year)
   - 17% discount vs monthly
   - Same features as monthly

### Team/Business Plans
1. **Team 20 Monthly**: $119/month (20 users)
   - **Cost per user**: $5.95/month
   
2. **Team 20 Annual**: $99/month (20 users, billed annually)
   - **Cost per user**: $4.95/month
   - Team branding with custom logo
   - Team management tools
   
3. **Team 50 Monthly**: $199/month (50 users)
   - **Cost per user**: $3.98/month
   
4. **Custom Enterprise**: Contact for pricing (50+ users)

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscription Memberships (Primary)
- Recurring monthly/annual revenue
- Multiple tiers (individual, team, enterprise)
- 30-day risk-free trial to reduce friction

### 2. Physical Products
- Barista tools (tampers, cupping bowls)
- Premium equipment designed by Matt Perger
- Stackable, lightweight, professional-grade

### 3. Gift Cards
- Prepaid memberships as gifts
- Corporate gifting potential

### 4. Coaching Services
- "BH Coaching" - premium 1-on-1 or group training
- Global network of certified coaches
- Higher-tier service

## Key Success Factors

1. **Authority & Credibility**
   - World champion founder
   - Science-based approach
   - Professional certifications

2. **Freemium Model**
   - Free tools and content drive awareness
   - Premium courses behind paywall

3. **B2B Focus**
   - Team plans at $5/user/month
   - Coffee shop staff training
   - Scalable revenue

4. **Certification Value**
   - Publicly visible credentials
   - Career capital for baristas
   - Employer validation

5. **Community & Network**
   - Global coaching network
   - Events and lectures
   - Professional community

## Estimated Revenue Model
- **Individual users**: $18/month × users
- **Team users**: $5-6/user/month × users
- **Physical products**: One-time purchases
- **Coaching**: Premium hourly/session rates

## Lessons for Coffee Craft
1. **Subscription works** for coffee education
2. **B2B opportunity** is significant (team plans)
3. **Free content** drives paid conversions
4. **Certifications** add perceived value
5. **Multi-tier pricing** captures different segments
